## Hi, I'm Stella 👋

I'm an AI & Machine Learning Researcher, Data Scientist, and Software Engineer with 10+ years of experience building intelligent systems, scalable data platforms, and machine learning solutions for health research and real-world applications.

I hold a BSc in Software Engineering from Makerere University and an MSc in Data Science from Manipal Academy of Higher Education. My work combines software engineering, machine learning, statistical modelling, and data engineering to build reliable, production-ready systems.

I've contributed to research and engineering projects at Uganda Virus Research Institute (UVRI), Makerere University Joint AIDS Program (MJAP), and MRC/UVRI & London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine (LSHTM), collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to solve complex data-intensive problems.
